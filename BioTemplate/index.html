Gigi Chao

Biography

Hong Kong business woman and activist.

Where did she go to school? The University of Manchester's Manchester School of Architecture.

Where does she work? Cheuk Nang (Holdings) Ltd., as vice-chair.

What are her accomplishments?

  • Founded the organisation Hong Kong Marriage Equality.
  • Wrote an early thesis on Marriage Equality.
  • Graduated with honors.
  • First female President of the Hong Kong Aviation Club.
